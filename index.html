About

Now celebrating our 21st year; this family-run business offers a friendly and efficient service that aims to provide complete customer satisfaction. In fact, most of our business comes via customer recommendation.

Keraion Tuning's critically-acclaimed service and upgrade centre ensures our customer’s vehicles are looked after by skilled and experienced technicians. Our parts department provides the very best products for our retail and trade customers worldwide.

We’re trusted by Formula 1 bosses, Top Gear presenters, WTCC World Champions, Olympic Gold Medalists, motoring journalists, and track day enthusiasts from all over the world. You can be sure that your car is in the safest of hands.

Please browse through our site to discover our products and services. If you have any further questions or would like to discuss anything not covered by our site, please contact us.

Servicing

We offer comprehensive servicing for performance vehicles from Audi, BMW, Ford, Ferrari, Nissan, Mercedes, Porsche and Subaru.

We employ some of the very best manufacturer-trained Master Technicians from Audi, Nissan, Mercedes and Subaru. This, combined with our training policies, a passion for motorsport and the best vehicle engineering, means our staff are some of the most diverse and skilled in the industry.

We use genuine or enhanced parts wherever possible, along with the finest lubricants from industry leaders Castrol, Mobil and Motul. Consistent investment in the correct diagnostic equipment and state of the art tools, ensures that manufacturer standards are exceeded and warranties are retained.

For customers attending track days, we can tailor a service package to include any extras you may need, such as additional transmission fluid changes, corner weighting or detailed laser alignment.

Performance & Upgrades

We have a diverse understanding of what makes the very best performance cars. Our reputation was originally garnered for our critically-acclaimed upgrades to the Subaru range and, more recently, the Nissan GT-R. The vast experience gained from years of successful operation has allowed us to invest in the very best facilities, training and equipment. Commercial success has seen us forge strong partnerships with the best brands in the industry and build strong relationships with the finest engineering firms in the UK.

We regularly tune models from Audi, BMW, Ferrari, Ford, Nissan, Mercedes, Porsche, Subaru and VW.

From simple upgrades to bespoke solutions for your vehicle; we offer everything you need under one roof.
